## §2.1 Database — DONE (provisioned from scratch)
- **Provider research** (free Postgres): chose **Neon free tier**. Decisive
  factor: repo uses `@neondatabase/serverless` (`neon()` HTTP + `Pool`
  WebSocket) as its ONLY driver — every non-Neon option (Supabase, Railway,
  CockroachDB, Render) would force a `pg`/`postgres.js` rewrite. Neon free =
  0.5 GB storage, 100 CU-hrs/mo, scale-to-zero, no inactivity deletion → fine
  for a pre-launch brand site. (Supabase pauses free projects after 7 days;
  Render deletes free DBs — both disqualified beyond the driver issue.)
