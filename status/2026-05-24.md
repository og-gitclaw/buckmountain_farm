## Mid-sprint clarification (Brendon)

**QR codes are authenticity stickers, not jar trackers (for v1).** Printed
in sheets of 50–75+. Photoshop team designs the sheets. Openclaw watches
the synced folder via Tailscale 24/7 and POSTs decoded tokens to the
backend. Tokens are pre-allocated server-side to guarantee no collisions
and to flag counterfeits the moment they appear.

`batch_id` on `qr_tokens` is now nullable. v2 will link tokens to batches
when we move to per-jar product tracking.
